The story of PM SHRI GSS MUS

Knowledge Discipline Service

Background

Rooted in public education and community trust.

PM SHRI GSS MUS has grown as an important center of learning for the local community, serving generations of students with dedication and care. The school was established to make quality education accessible, to encourage discipline, and to prepare young learners for responsible citizenship.

As the school developed, it expanded its academic support, facilities, co-curricular activities, and student leadership opportunities. Today, it continues to uphold its founding principles while embracing modern teaching practices and PM SHRI-aligned improvements.

Founding Principles

  • Education for every child
  • Discipline with dignity
  • Respect for community and nation
  • Equal opportunity and inclusion
  • Learning beyond the classroom
